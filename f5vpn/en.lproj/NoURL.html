The VPN login URL is not set.

Please consult with your system administrator for the login URL. The login URL is typically in the form of https://host.name/tp.

Once you got your login URL, please follow the procedure below to set the URL.

  1. Open Terminal application.
  2. Type the following command and hit Enter key: 
    defaults write ec.koji.f5vpn LoginURL your-login-URL
    where your-login-URL is the URL you got from your system administrator.
